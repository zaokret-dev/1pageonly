FAQ
Questions?
We've Got Answers.
Do I need to know anything about websites or technology?+

Not at all. You know your trade — we handle everything else. All we need from you is a 15-minute phone call. We take care of the design, the setup, and getting you live. Zero technical knowledge required.

How long does it take to get my website live?+

We deliver your site for review within 48–72 hours. After you approve it (or request any changes), we go live within 24 hours. Most clients are live within 5 business days from the first call.

Are there any monthly fees after I pay the one-time fee?+

No monthly fees from us — ever. The only ongoing cost is your domain name, which runs about $10–15 per year, paid directly to the domain provider. Hosting is free. No surprises.

How quickly will I start getting calls from my website?+

It varies, but most clients start seeing organic traffic within 4–8 weeks. If you pair your site with a Google Business Profile (which we help you set up), results often come even faster. Some clients get their first call within the first week.

What information do you need from me to get started?+

Just your business name, phone number, service area, and a list of your services. If you have photos of your work, even better — but not required. We can start with just the basics and build from there.

What if I'm not happy with the website?+

We offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. We send you a preview before going live, and you can request as many changes as you need. We don't consider the job done until you love it.

Do you work with all types of tradesmen?+

Yes — plumbers, electricians, painters, handymen, roofers, HVAC technicians, landscapers, carpenters, remodelers and more. If you work with your hands and want more customers, we've got you covered. We work with businesses across all 50 states.
